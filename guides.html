Disaster Safety Tips

FLOOD SAFETY TIPS

What to do before, during, and after a flood:

  • Stay indoors & be calm
  • Prepare an emergency supply
  • Have an emergency supply kit
  • Disconnect utilities
  • Stay only for essential items
  • During flooding, stay indoors

VOLCANO SAFETY TIPS

What to do in case of a volcanic eruption:

  • Prepare to evacuate if needed
  • Have an emergency supply kit
  • Know your evacuation routes
  • During an eruption, stay indoors
  • Protect against falling ash
  • Listen for warnings and updates

TYPHOON SAFETY TIPS

What to do in case of a typhoon:

  • Stay indoors & be calm
  • Secure loose objects
  • Monitor weather conditions
  • Avoid low-lying areas
  • Move to a safer location if needed
  • Use available utilities if flooding

EARTHQUAKE SAFETY TIPS

What to do before, during, and after an earthquake:

  • Secure heavy objects
  • Plan for an emergency
  • Keep emergency supplies ready
  • Be careful of aftershocks
  • Stay indoors during shaking
  • Check for injuries and damage
